Good to know

Frequently asked questions.

Answers to what we're asked most. Can't find what you need? Just give us a call.

What are your office hours?

Monday–Friday 10:00 AM–6:00 PM, Sunday 10:00 AM–4:00 PM, closed Saturdays. Oral surgery and orthodontics are available on Tuesdays.

Do you accept my insurance?

We work with most major dental plans. See our full list of accepted insurance — if you don't see yours, give us a call and we'll check.

Do you take walk-ins?

Yes — while we recommend booking ahead, walk-ins are welcome. You can check in as a walk-in right when you arrive.

What should I bring to my first visit?

A photo ID, your insurance card if you have one, and a list of any current medications. If you have records from a previous dentist, feel free to bring those too.

How do I book, check in, or pay online?

All three are available right from our homepage — book an appointment, check in for one you already have, or pay a balance with your invoice code.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Just give us a call as early as you can, and we'll help you find a new time.

Do you treat children?

Yes — our pediatric dentistry care is built around younger patients, from their very first visit onward.
